## Objective
I needed a simple way to stack landscape pictures into a vertical format.

## Origin Context
Many tools already exist, but they often include too many options, distracting banners, and hard-to-use interfaces.

## What This Experiment Does
stk focuses on a direct workflow: pick images, stack them quickly, and produce a clean vertical composition.
