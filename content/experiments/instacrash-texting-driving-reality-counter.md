## Mission Objective
Push back against the bad habit of driving and texting with an interactive experience.

## Intelligence Background
People can fall into overgeneralizations like "everyone is texting while driving" or "all women are texting while driving."

## Operation Summary
instacrash provides a statistical track of reality, helping users compare assumptions against measured proportions and context.
